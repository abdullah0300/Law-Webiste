We provide Company Registration Services from inception of the business to its dissolution. Amongst others we provide Company registration services, annual and routine company compliance and any other ad-hoc services the company may require.

Company Registration

In connection with company registration (setting up services), our work encompasses the following:

01

BRELA - Company Registration

Incorporation of a subsidiary or registration of a branch office with the Business Registration and Licensing Authority (BRELA).

  • Subsidiary incorporation
  • Branch office registration
  • Trust registration
  • NGO registration
  • Societies & Partnerships
Contact Us
02

TRA - TIN & VAT Registrations

Registration with the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) and obtaining a TIN (Taxpayers Identification Number) and VAT Certificate.

  • TIN registration and acquisition
  • VAT Certificate processing
  • Tax compliance setup
  • TRA system onboarding
Contact Us
03

Tax Clearance Certificate

Processing the Tax Clearance Certificate(s) with TRA to ensure compliance with tax regulations.

  • Application preparation and submission
  • Document compilation
  • TRA liaison and follow-up
  • Certificate collection and delivery
Contact Us
04

Business Licence

Applying for business licence(s) with either BRELA or Municipal Council as the case may be.

  • BRELA business licenses
  • Municipal Council licenses
  • Sector-specific licenses
  • Annual renewal services
Contact Us
05

Bank Account Opening

Assisting with opening of bank account(s) for your newly registered company.

  • Bank selection guidance
  • Document preparation
  • Bank introduction and liaison
  • Account activation support
Contact Us
06

Professional Registrations

Processing registration(s) with professional or regulatory bodies and government departments.

  • Contractors Registration Board (CRB)
  • Tanzania Investment Centre (TIC)
  • Export Processing Zone (EPZ)
  • Special Economic Zone (SEZ)
  • Professional organizations
Contact Us

Liquidation & Deregistration Services

Complete support for winding up operations and deregistering entities in Tanzania.

01

Company Liquidation

Liquidation of a Company in Tanzania (with BRELA) and deregistration with TRA (for TIN and VAT registrations).

  • Liquidation documentation preparation
  • BRELA liquidation filing
  • TRA deregistration process
  • Final tax clearance
Contact Us
02

Striking Off Defunct Companies

Striking off from the Company registry the defunct companies that are no longer operational.

  • Eligibility assessment
  • Application preparation
  • Regulatory submissions
  • Registry removal confirmation
Contact Us
03

Branch Deregistration

Deregistration of a Branch of a foreign company (with BRELA, and with TRA for TIN and VAT registrations).

  • Branch closure documentation
  • BRELA deregistration filing
  • TRA tax deregistration
  • Final compliance clearance
