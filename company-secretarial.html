Company Secretarial Services

Complete company registration and compliance support from inception to dissolution

We provide Company Registration Services from inception of the business to its dissolution. Amongst others we provide Company registration services, annual and routine company compliance and any other ad-hoc services the company may require.

A: Company Registration

In connection with company registration (setting up services), our work encompasses the following:

01

BRELA - Company Registration

Incorporation of a subsidiary or registration of a branch office with the Business Registration and Licensing Authority (BRELA); or any types of entities (Trust, NGO's, Societies, Partnerships, etc.) with the respective regulatory authorities.

02

TRA - TIN & VAT Registrations

Registration with the Tanzania Revenue Authority (TRA) and obtaining a TIN (Taxpayers Identification Number) and where required, Value Added Tax (VAT) Certificate.

03

Tax Clearance Certificate

Processing the Tax Clearance Certificate(s) with TRA to ensure compliance with tax regulations.

04

Business Licence

Applying for business licence(s) with either BRELA or Municipal Council as the case may be.

05

Bank Account Opening

Assisting with opening of bank account(s) for your newly registered company.

06

Professional Registrations

Processing registration(s) with professional or regulatory bodies including:

  • Contractors Registration Board (CRB)
  • Tanzania Investment Centre (TIC)
  • Export Processing Zone (EPZ)
  • Special Economic Zone (SEZ)
  • Government departments and professional organisations
  • Other registrations as required

B: Company Administration & Compliance

In connection with company administration and compliance, our services comprise of the following:

01

AGM/EGM & Board Meetings

Organizing and attending the Annual General Meeting (AGM) or the Extraordinary Annual General Meeting (EGM) and Board of Directors (BOD) Meetings and record matters deliberated thereof on behalf of the Company Secretary.

02

Annual Return Filing

Completing and filing of annual return (once in each calendar year) with BRELA to maintain company compliance.

03

Financial Statements Filing

Filing with BRELA the audited financial statements of the company as required by law.

04

Tax Health Check

Tax Health check / due diligence for Companies operating in Tanzania to ensure ongoing compliance.

05

Registered Business Address

Providing registered business address to our clients for official company correspondence and legal requirements.

C: Liquidation & Deregistration Services

01

Company Liquidation

Liquidation of a Company in Tanzania (with BRELA) and deregistration with TRA (for TIN and VAT registrations).

02

Striking Off Defunct Companies

Striking off from the Company registry the defunct companies that are no longer operational.

03

Branch Deregistration

Deregistration of a Branch of a foreign company (with BRELA, and with TRA for TIN and VAT registrations).
