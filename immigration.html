Immigration Services

Expert immigration solutions for businesses and individuals

Immigration and tax regulations are becoming increasingly complex and interlinked and can no longer be addressed as independent disciplines. This is most evident in regulations affecting international travellers.

Our immigration and legal experts conduct pre-travel assessments to organizations to effectively manage immigration requirements against the laws of Tanzania and take appropriate action to minimize potential consequences.

01

Work & Residence Permit Applications

Based on the Non-Citizens (Employment Regulation) Act 2015, a non-Tanzania citizen has to first apply to the Ministry of Labour for a work permit (Class A, B or C depending on the circumstances) and once approved, then apply for a residence permit with the Immigration Authority.

Our Services Include:

  • Compiling information required for permit applications
  • Drafting and submitting applications to authorities
  • Following up to ensure permits are obtained on time
  • Addressing any issues that may arise
  • Endorsement of permits into passport
  • Advisory on work permit matters
02

Special & Dependents Pass

We assist clients with compiling information required for application for special/dependents passes for the dependents to the non-citizens investors/employees as per the requirement of the Immigration regulations.

  • Application drafting and submission to Ministry of Home Affairs
  • Follow-up with Immigration authorities
  • Ensuring timely approval
  • Advisory on special pass matters

Eligible dependents: Spouse and children under 18 years within a formal marriage.

03

Visa Applications

We assist clients with compiling information required for various visa types to be obtained at arrival or at the assignee's home country.

04

Registration with Professional Boards

We assist clients with registration to various professional boards including NBAA, ERB, CRB, PSPTB, TLS, TRA, etc.

  • Collecting all necessary information for registration
  • Drafting and submitting applications
  • Follow-ups with professional boards
  • Addressing issues to ensure timely registration
05

NIDA Registration Processes

We assist clients with NIDA ID application and registration processes for non-citizens employees.

  • Filling NIDA ID application forms
  • Getting forms signed by local government authorities
  • Submitting applications to relevant NIDA offices
  • Arranging and escorting employees for biometric scanning
  • Collecting IDs once available
06

Immigration Return for Employment of Non-Citizens

We collect all necessary information and assist clients with preparation and filing of annual return for employment of non-citizens employees before the deadline (31st March) to the Ministry of Home Affairs (Immigration Division).

07

Bi-Annual Labour Returns

We collect all necessary information and assist clients with preparation and filing of bi-annual return for employment of non-citizens employees before deadlines (30th June & 31st December) to the Ministry of Labour.

08

Trainings & Seminars

We provide comprehensive training sessions and seminars on immigration regulations, compliance requirements, and best practices for managing cross-border assignments.
