What We Offer

Comprehensive Immigration Support

01

Work & Residence Permit Applications

Based on the Non-Citizens (Employment Regulation) Act 2015, we handle complete work permit (Class A, B or C) and residence permit applications with the Ministry of Labour and Immigration Authority in Tanzania.

  • Compiling required information
  • Application drafting & submission
  • Timely follow-up with authorities
  • Obtaining the permits once approved and sharing them with you
  • Endorsement of the permits in the passports
  • Advisory on work and residence permit matters
Contact Us
02

Special & Dependents Pass

Complete assistance for special and dependents passes for family members of non-citizen investors and employees as per Immigration regulations.

  • Application to Ministry of Home Affairs
  • Follow-up with Immigration authorities
  • Ensuring timely approval
  • Advisory on special pass matters

Eligible dependents: Spouse within a formal marriage and children under 18 years of age.
