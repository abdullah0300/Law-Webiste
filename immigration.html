Our Expertise

Seamless Immigration
Solutions

Immigration and tax regulations are becoming increasingly complex and interlinked and can no longer be addressed as independent disciplines. This is most evident in regulations affecting international travellers.

Our immigration and legal experts conduct pre-travel assessments to organizations to effectively manage immigration requirements against the laws of Tanzania and take appropriate action to minimize potential consequences.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Immigration Support

01

Work & Residence Permit Applications

Based on the Non-Citizens (Employment Regulation) Act 2015, we handle complete work permit (Class A, B or C) and residence permit applications with the Immigration Authority.

  • Compiling required information
  • Application drafting & submission
  • Timely follow-up with authorities
  • Passport endorsement services
  • Advisory on work permit matters
Contact Us
02

Special & Dependents Pass

Complete assistance for special and dependents passes for family members of non-citizen investors and employees as per Immigration regulations.

  • Application to Ministry of Home Affairs
  • Follow-up with Immigration authorities
  • Ensuring timely approval
  • Advisory on special pass matters

Eligible dependents: Spouse and children under 18 years within a formal marriage.

Contact Us

  • Visa on Arrival Support

Registration with Professional Boards
