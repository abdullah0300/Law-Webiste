Legal Services

Comprehensive legal solutions for businesses and individuals

Our lawyers and consultants understand the increasingly complex legal, regulatory and commercial risks of the economy and provide holistic guidance around strategic decisions that meets the needs of today's business.

We pride ourselves with our lawyers with various backgrounds who provide integrated and comprehensive pertinent services our clients can trust. We service local and foreign multinational companies in different sectors on a wide variety of legal matters.

Today's lenders and borrowers face countless challenges, including increasing complexity of financial services regulation across the world. Our lawyers are particularly noted for their proficiency in acquisition finance, project finance, restructuring and insolvency, financial services regulation, structured finance, trade and export finance, real estate finance and securitization.
