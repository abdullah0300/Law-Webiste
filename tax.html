Tax Services

Expert tax strategy, planning and compliance services

Serena Consulting is the leading tax practice in Tanzania. Our focus is tax strategy, planning and compliance. We do not just give you advice, we assist you to implement it.

A: Initial Set Up Tax Advice

To ensure smooth operations for our clients we provide a detailed legal and tax analysis of the best option to set up a business in Tanzania, the possible Tanzanian tax consequences and tax compliance obligations of the chosen structure. We also advise on feasibility for tax efficient funding of Tanzanian operations, tax incentives, repatriation of profits and the exit structure.

B: Tax Compliance Services

Compliance and reporting make huge demands on tax and finance functions. We understand that what you need is access, visibility and control. Our market-leading approach combines extensive local compliance, legal and accounting experience with a standard business compliance process.

01

Corporate Tax Compliance

Provisional - Computations and Returns

We remind, advise and assist our clients with the preparation of the quarterly provisional tax estimates as per the requirements of the Income Tax Act (ITA), 2004. This includes obtaining the relevant information from our clients for preparing the provisional tax computation and filing the tax returns with TRA after the clients' approval.

Annual Corporate Income Tax Returns

Carrying out a review of the Company's audited financial statements and preparing a tax computation to determine the Company's taxable income for the year of income in accordance with the provisions of ITA, 2004. We also assist companies to prepare the final tax return and submit the tax return to TRA after the company's approval and signature.

02

Value Added Tax (VAT)

In accordance with the VAT Act, 2014 we prepare VAT returns on a monthly basis for our clients and file VAT returns online to TRA on or before the due date, based on the approved VAT computations by our clients.

  • Monthly VAT return preparation and filing
  • Processing through ESD/EFD machine all invoices issued
  • Submission of daily Z reports to TRA
  • VAT verification for refunds
03

Withholding Tax

Monthly Returns

We confirm WHT payments to be made on a monthly basis, based on approved schedules finalized and signed off by the Company and file monthly WHT returns based on the approved tax computations.

Bi-Annual Returns

We assist companies to file WHT bi-annual returns by the due dates and review of WHT certificates issued to payees/vendors.

04

Remittance of Payroll Taxes

We assist our clients with preparation of monthly payroll taxes namely PAYE (income tax), Skills and Development Levy (SDL) and Social Security Contributions (NSSF), Workers Compensation Fund (WCF); and determine the net pay.

  • Monthly SDL and WCF returns filing
  • Six monthly PAYE and SDL returns with TRA
  • Issue PAYE certificates to employees annually or as required
05

City Service Levy

We assist companies in preparation of the schedules for the levy on quarterly basis and submit for your review and approval and payment of the levy to the city/municipal council.

06

Transfer Pricing (TP)

The global nature of business means there is a constant flow of products, intangibles, services and financial instruments moving across borders and business units. With increased regulatory scrutiny, companies are now required to deliver detailed TP analysis and documentation for each of these items, which can be both time-consuming and complex.

Our professionals are helping companies traverse the web of country tax laws, regulations, rulings, methods and requirements to develop a complete TP system, allowing companies to reduce risk and cost and be more efficient in the market.

C: Tax Advisory Services

Our tax advisory professionals can assist companies with alignment of their tax function with their business operations and strategy, transaction planning and support, and enterprise risk management activities.

01

On-Call Tax Advisory Services

We advise our clients on-call tax advisory services with respect to tax consequences on contemplated transactions related to their activities in Tanzania (or abroad); and provide a generic or specific advice on:

  • Corporate income tax
  • VAT, customs duty
  • Withholding tax
  • Transfer pricing
  • Employment tax issues
  • Direct and indirect taxes
  • Local taxes
02

TRA Audit Support

We assist our clients with TRA tax audits including reviewing the records, documents and TRA's tax audit findings and workings to ascertain the correctness of the findings and the amount computed.

  • Review of audit findings and workings
  • Arrange and attend meetings with TRA
  • Sort out issues and queries on behalf of clients
  • Negotiate favorable settlement of tax liability
03

Appeals Against Tax Assessments

We follow up on matters and proceedings pending at the Tax Revenue Appeals Board, Tribunal or the Court of Appeal and represent our clients as necessary.

  • Preparation of notices of intention to appeal
  • Filing statements of appeal against tax assessments
  • Attending hearing sessions on behalf of clients
  • Preparing written submissions with legal arguments
  • Obtaining and sharing written judgments
