Our Tax Services

Our tax services cover every stage of your business lifecycle

1. Direct & Indirect Tax Compliance in Tanzania

At Serena Attorneys, we provide expert compliance services for direct and indirect taxes affecting businesses in Tanzania. Our team ensures accurate preparation, review, and submission of tax returns for corporate income tax, withholding taxes, PAYE, SDL, NSSF, WCF, City Service Levy, Transfer Pricing as well as compliance with indirect taxes such as VAT, excise duty, and customs duties. We analyse accounting records, validate draft returns, and identify potential deductions or misclassifications using advanced techniques. We help businesses minimize risks, optimize tax positions, and maintain full regulatory compliance under Tanzanian tax laws.

2. Tax Consulting, Planning & Structuring

We deliver domestic and international tax planning solutions tailored to your business needs. Our services include advising on capital structures, cross-border financing, treasury solutions, hybrid investments, and derivatives. We provide detailed tax opinions, technical guidance, and compliance support to ensure your organization remains tax-efficient and fully compliant.

3. M&A Tax Advisory & Deal Structuring

Our team offers tax advisory for mergers and acquisitions, ensuring tax-efficient deal structuring for corporate buyers, financial investors, and vendors. We assess tax risks, advise on transaction financing, shareholder treatment, and post-deal integration. From indirect tax issues to deductibility of transaction costs, we help you minimize tax exposure and maximize value in every transaction.

4. Tax Dispute Resolution

We offer dispute resolution services, assisting clients with tax audits, assessments, and appeals. Our team handles objections, negotiates with tax authorities, and defends your tax position effectively. Whether facing investigations or complex compliance challenges, we ensure your interests are protected with expert legal and tax guidance.
